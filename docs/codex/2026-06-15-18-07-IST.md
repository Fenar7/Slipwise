## 2. Completed Milestones

### A. Document Labels & Currency Handling
1. **Net Amount Payable Label**:
   * Renamed the label `Amount Payable` / `Amount payable` to **`Net Amount Payable`** across all templates and document frames.
   * Hardcoded currency outputs remain strictly set to Indian Rupees (`â‚ą` / `INR`), using `en-IN` formatting.
   * Prefixed generated amount-in-words text strings with `"Rupees"` (e.g. `Rupees eight thousand only`).

### B. Business Salutations (From Section)
1. **Schema Extension**:
   * Added `salutation: z.string().trim().optional()` to the branding configuration schema inside `schema.ts`.
2. **UI Dropdown Selection**:
   * Added a dropdown SelectField in the Form Sidebar under **Business Details** in `invoice-workspace.tsx`.
   * Options: `None` (empty), `M/s.`, `Mr.`, `Ms.`, `Mrs.`, and `Dr.`
3. **Canvas & Template Rendering**:
   * Integrated the salutation prefix in the workspace header block and the "From" sections of all five templates and the fallback preview canvas.
   * Rendered dynamically next to the company name in both edit mode (canvas) and preview/print mode.

### C. Signature / Approval Blocks
1. **Renaming & Schema Extension**:
   * Renamed the bottom signature block section from "Authorized by" to **"Approved By"**.
   * Upgraded `schema.ts` to make the primary `authorizedBy` name field completely **optional** for flexibility.
   * Added optional fields `authorizedByDesignation` and `authorizedByCompany` to schemas and types.
2. **Form Workspace Integration**:
   * Configured three-column inputs in the Sidebar under the **Signature** block: Approved by (Name), Designation, and Company name.
3. **Template & Inline Edit Canvas Sizing**:
   * Rendered all three optional inputs in the signatory section in preview mode and as inline text fields in edit mode.
   * Adjusted styling to make Designation and Company Name text size `text-sm` (14px) and increased color contrast/opacity to ensure high readability.

### D. Classical Bordered Invoice Number Sizing
1. **Sizing and Font Weight Adjustment**:
   * Reduced the font size from `text-2xl` to `text-xl`.
   * Adjusted font weight from `font-bold` to `font-semibold` to match the modern aesthetic of other templates.
